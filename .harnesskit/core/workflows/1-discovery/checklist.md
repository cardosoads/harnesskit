# Validation Checklist: Project Brief

Validate each item below against the generated brief. Items marked as **required** must pass for the brief to be approved. Optional items are recommended but do not block approval.

---

## Required Items

- [ ] **Clear description** — Does the project have a concise description (2-5 sentences) that anyone can understand without additional context?
  - Criterion: a non-technical person would read it and understand what the project does.

- [ ] **Problem defined** — Is the problem the project solves explicitly stated with the target audience identified?
  - Criterion: the sentence "solves problem X for audience Y" can be extracted from the text.

- [ ] **At least 1 user profile** — Is there at least one user profile with a description and needs?
  - Criterion: the profile has a name/type, description, and at least one listed need.

- [ ] **Scope with boundaries** — Does the brief define what is IN scope and what is OUT of scope?
  - Criterion: both lists exist and have at least 1 item each.

- [ ] **No ambiguities** — Is the brief free of contradictions or vague terms without definition?
  - Criterion: there are no contradictory statements; technical terms are explained or contextualized.

---

## Optional Items (recommended)

- [ ] **Success metrics** — Were measurable metrics defined to evaluate the project's success?
  - Criterion: at least one metric with a clear indicator (e.g., "500 active users in the first month").

- [ ] **Constraints documented** — Are technical, timeline, or budget constraints recorded?
  - Criterion: constraints listed with their impact described.

- [ ] **References provided** — Are there references or inspirations that help understand the project's direction?
  - Criterion: at least one product, tool, or example mentioned.

---

## Validation Result

- **All required items approved?** -> Brief approved. Proceed to handoff.
- **Any required item failed?** -> Inform the user which item failed and go back to the corresponding step to fix it.
- **Optional items failed?** -> Inform the user, suggest filling them in, but do not block progress.
