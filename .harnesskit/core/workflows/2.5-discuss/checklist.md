# Checklist: Implementation Vision

## Required Items

- [ ] **Tech preferences captured** — At least one technology preference is stated (language, framework, or database)
- [ ] **Architecture preferences stated** — A clear architectural pattern or approach is defined
- [ ] **Constraints documented** — Team size, deployment environment, or infrastructure constraints are recorded
- [ ] **Quality priorities ranked** — At least top 3 quality priorities are ranked with rationale
- [ ] **Key decisions documented** — At least one key decision is recorded in the decisions table

## Optional Items

- [ ] **Gray areas explored** — Ambiguities in requirements have been identified and addressed (or flagged for architecture)
- [ ] **Inspirations listed** — Reference systems or codebases are documented with what to take from each
- [ ] **Compliance requirements noted** — Any regulatory or security requirements are captured
- [ ] **Budget and timeline stated** — Budget constraints and timeline expectations are documented
